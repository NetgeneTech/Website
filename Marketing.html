What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing uses online channels and data to build brand awareness, attract customers, and drive conversions. Services include search engine optimization (SEO), paid advertising (PPC), social media, content marketing, email campaigns, and analytics-driven optimizations to maximize ROI.
