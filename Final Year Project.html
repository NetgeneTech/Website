What We Offer for Final Year Projects

Netgene Technologies helps students deliver successful final year projects with hands-on mentorship, structured milestones, practical prototypes, and professional documentation. We cover engineering, software, embedded, robotics, AI/ML, and interdisciplinary projects.

Project Services

Project Ideation & Topic Selection

Personalized topic brainstorming aligned to your branch, interests, and academic scope — novelty checks and feasibility analysis.

Project Plan & Milestones

Detailed timeline with weekly deliverables, resource list, Gantt chart, and task assignments to keep you on track.

Design & Simulations

CAD modelling, FEA, circuit design, algorithm design and simulation using tools like Fusion360, SolidWorks, MATLAB, Ansys, and Proteus.

Prototype Development

Hardware prototyping, PCB design, microcontroller programming, sensor integration, and 3D printing support for working demos.

Software & Mobile Apps

Full-stack development, mobile apps, cloud/backend APIs, database design, and UI/UX for project interfaces and dashboards.

Testing & Validation

Functional testing, performance evaluation, data collection, optimization, and test report preparation for submission.

Report & Documentation

Professional project report (IEEE/College format), concise abstract, block diagrams, flowcharts, and user manuals ready for submission.

Viva & Presentation Prep

Slide deck creation, mock viva sessions, probable question sets, and tips to present confidently before examiners.

Publication & Patent Guidance

Support for writing conference/journal papers, submission guidance, and basic patent filing advice for novel projects.
