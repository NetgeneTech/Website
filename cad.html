Course Modules / What We Cover

CAD — 2D & 3D Modelling

Parametric modelling, assemblies, sheet metal, surface modelling, drawings and drafting using SolidWorks, CATIA & AutoCAD.

CAM — CNC Programming

Toolpath generation, post-processing, 2.5D & 3D milling, turning, multi-axis basics, G-code understanding and CNC simulation using Mastercam / Fusion CAM.

CAE — FEA & Simulation

Structural analysis, thermal, modal, optimization, meshing strategies, result interpretation using ANSYS / Abaqus and design validation workflows.

Design for Manufacturing (DFM)

Manufacturability checks, tolerance analysis, assembly constraints, cost-effective design choices and rapid prototyping considerations.

Reverse Engineering & Scanning

Point cloud processing, reverse modelling workflows and converting scanned data into CAD-ready parts for refurbishment or redesign.

3D Printing & Prototyping

FDM/SLA printing fundamentals, slicing best practices, post-processing, and creating prints directly from CAD models for validation.

Project-Based Learning

End-to-end mini projects: design, simulate, manufacture (CNC/3D print), test and document — portfolio-ready deliverables for students.

Industry Tools & Workflows

Version control for CAD, PDM basics, collaboration workflows, BOM generation and integration with CAM/CAE pipelines.

Certification & Placement Support

Course completion certificate, project certificate, mock interviews, and introductions to industry partners for internships and placements.
